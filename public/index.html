About

For more than half a decade, we’ve been performing together as a group of friends, bringing music and light to dozens of simchas and events, and raising tens of thousands for our wonderful charities.

Having initially performed as The 4 Sons, we relaunched as Kol Tov when we started university in 2018. This name represents what we’re all about – not just high quality music, but also using our ‘Kolot’ (voices) in another way to share our central message wherever we go – that you can take something you love and use it to make the world a better place.

Find out how Kol Tov can enhance your simcha

Our unique combination of youthful energy and wide-ranging experience has brought to life countless simchas and other events. From our atmospheric Shabbat melodies to our awesome a cappella arrangements, we bring our mission of ‘making a difference through music’ not only to the beneficiaries of our charities, but also to every guest who hears us perform.

We’re proud to be making a difference through music

Charity has always been at the heart of what we do. We are fortunate that in doing something we love, we have been able to change thousands of lives for the better, thanks to the remarkable work of our chosen causes: Chai Cancer Care, Emunah and WaterAid.

Together, these three charities represent our responsibilities towards the Jewish community, Israel, and humanity at large. We have close personal connections to each of these charities and believe strongly in what they do. Hover over the images below to find out more.

WaterAid

WaterAid is an international not-for-profit, determined to make clean water, decent toilets and good hygiene normal for everyone, everywhere within a generation. Only by tackling these three essentials in ways that last can many people around the world change their lives for good.

WaterAid

Chai Cancer Care

Chai Cancer Care provides an ever increasing range of comprehensive, professional and expert services to any member of the community affected by cancer. This includes counselling, complementary therapies, palliative care and home support for patients, their families and friends.

Chai Cancer Care

Emunah

Emunah helps thousands of children in Israel through its network of residential homes, high schools, day care centres and therapy and counselling centres. Emunah addresses the complex needs of those affected by abuse, ill-health, poverty and violence in order to break the cycle of disadvantage and improve their lives.

Emunah
